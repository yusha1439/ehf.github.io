Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 18, 2025  |  Effective: June 18, 2025

📱 SMS & Mobile Data — Key Commitment

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent — this information will not be shared with any third parties. SMS opt-in data and consent will not be used for any purpose beyond the program for which you explicitly enrolled.

1. Who We Are

Estes Heritage Foundation, Inc. ("Foundation," "we," "us," or "our") is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (Federal EIN: 81-1482687) dedicated to sharing the message of Islam through digital media, television broadcasting, and educational websites. Our principal website is estesheritagefoundation.org.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, sign up for SMS communications, or otherwise interact with us.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Name and email address (contact forms, donation inquiries)
  • Mobile phone number (SMS opt-in forms)
  • Donation amounts and related information
  • Any messages or correspondence you send us

Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring URLs
  • Device type and operating system

We do not use cookies for advertising tracking. Basic analytics may be collected to understand how visitors use our website and improve our content.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information only for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and correspondence
  • To process and acknowledge donations
  • To send SMS text messages only to individuals who have explicitly opted in to our messaging program
  • To provide updates about the Foundation's programs, events, and Islamic educational content
  • To comply with legal obligations applicable to nonprofit organizations
  • To improve our website and communications

4. SMS / Text Message Communications

Opt-In Consent

We only send SMS messages to individuals who have given explicit, prior written consent to receive them. Consent is collected through our website opt-in form or in person at Foundation events. Consent is not a condition of making a donation or receiving any service from the Foundation.

What You Will Receive

If you opt in to our SMS program, you may receive messages including:

  • Updates about Foundation programs and projects (Guide US TV, ShareIslam, etc.)
  • Islamic educational content and reminders
  • Event announcements and dawah campaign updates
  • Donation acknowledgments and campaign fundraising updates
  • Volunteer and participation opportunities

Message frequency may vary. You may receive up to 4–8 messages per month.

Message and data rates may apply. Contact your wireless carrier for details.

Opt-Out / Unsubscribe

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You may also reply STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT. Upon receipt of an opt-out request, we will send one final confirmation message and will not send further SMS messages to that number.

To re-subscribe after opting out, reply START or visit our website opt-in form.

Help

Reply HELP to any SMS message for assistance, or contact us directly at contact@guideus.tv.

Mobile Data — No Third-Party Sharing

We will never sell, rent, share, or disclose your mobile phone number or SMS opt-in consent to any third party, affiliate, or lead generator for marketing or promotional purposes. Your mobile information is used solely to deliver the SMS program you have enrolled in. Sharing of information with operational subcontractors (e.g., our SMS platform provider) is limited to what is necessary to deliver the service and is governed by data processing agreements.

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted vendors who assist us in operating our website, processing donations, or delivering SMS communications (e.g., Twilio for SMS delivery). These vendors are contractually prohibited from using your information for any purpose other than providing services to us.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Protection of Rights: We may share information to protect the rights, property, or safety of the Foundation, our users, or others.

We do not share your information with third-party marketers, advertisers, data brokers, or any entity for commercial purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy or as required by applicable law. SMS opt-in records are retained for a minimum of four (4) years to demonstrate compliance with carrier and regulatory requirements. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8).

7. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure. We encourage you to use caution when sharing personal information online.

8. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Opt-Out of SMS: Reply STOP to any text message at any time
  • Opt-Out of Email: Use the unsubscribe link in any email or contact us directly

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@guideus.tv.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website and SMS programs are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to third-party websites (including Guide US TV, ShareIslam, ReciteQuran, and other Foundation projects). This Privacy Policy applies only to our website. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website or participation in our SMS program after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Policy.

12. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the United States and applicable state law. The Foundation operates as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization registered in the United States (EIN: 81-1482687).
