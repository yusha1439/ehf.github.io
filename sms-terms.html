1. Program Description

The Estes Heritage Foundation, Inc. (Federal EIN: 81-1482687) operates an SMS text messaging program to share news, Islamic educational content, dawah campaign updates, donation opportunities, and event information with individuals who have opted in to receive messages.

This SMS program is operated by the Estes Heritage Foundation in support of its 501(c)(3) charitable mission — sharing the message of Islam through digital media, television, and educational outreach. Program messages may include:

  • Foundation news and program updates (Guide US TV, ShareIslam, ReciteQuran, etc.)
  • Islamic educational reminders and content
  • Fundraising and donation campaigns
  • Event announcements and dawah opportunities
  • Volunteer and community engagement updates

2. How to Opt In

You may opt in to receive SMS messages from the Estes Heritage Foundation by:

  • Completing the SMS opt-in form on our website and checking the consent checkbox
  • Providing your phone number and verbal or written consent at a Foundation event

By opting in, you confirm that:

  • You are the account holder or authorized user of the mobile number provided
  • You are 13 years of age or older
  • You consent to receive recurring automated text messages from Estes Heritage Foundation
  • You understand that consent is not required to donate or use any Foundation services

Upon opting in, you will receive a confirmation message: "You're subscribed to Estes Heritage Foundation SMS updates. Msg & data rates may apply. Msg frequency varies. Reply STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for help."

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You may receive up to 4–8 messages per month, depending on Foundation activity, events, and campaigns. We will not flood your inbox — messages are sent only when we have meaningful updates to share.

4. Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Standard messaging and data rates charged by your wireless carrier apply to all messages sent and received. Contact your wireless carrier for details about your plan. Estes Heritage Foundation does not charge any fees for SMS participation.

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

5. How to Opt Out

🛑 To Stop Receiving Messages — Reply STOP

You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Text STOP to the number from which you received a message. After sending STOP, you will receive one final SMS confirming your unsubscribe request, and no further messages will be sent to that number.

The following opt-out keywords are all recognized and honored:

  • STOP
  • STOPALL
  • UNSUBSCRIBE
  • CANCEL
  • END
  • QUIT
  • REVOKE

Opt-out confirmation message: "You have been unsubscribed from Estes Heritage Foundation SMS. No further messages will be sent. Reply START to re-subscribe. For help: contact@guideus.tv"

To re-subscribe after opting out, reply START to the same number, or complete the opt-in form on our website.
